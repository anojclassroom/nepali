
# Contributing

Thanks for helping improve this Nepali TV playlist!

## Guidelines
- Only submit links that are **official** or **publicly available** and legal to share.
- Prefer broadcaster‑hosted HLS (\.m3u8) or official YouTube/website links.
- Include a short description and source reference in your PR.

## How to test
- Load the updated M3U in Kodi / VLC.
- Verify playback for at least 60 seconds.
